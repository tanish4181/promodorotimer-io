Welcome to Your New Pomodoro Timer!

Here's a quick guide to help you get started on your journey to enhanced focus and productivity.

Core Features

Focus Sessions

Work in focused 25-minute intervals. You can customize the duration in the settings.

Short & Long Breaks

Take 5-minute short breaks after each session and a longer 15-minute break after four sessions.

Task Management

Add your to-do items directly in the timer to stay on track during your focus sessions.

Powerful Tools for Focus

Website Blocker

Block distracting websites during your focus sessions. Manage your blocklist in the settings.

YouTube Integration

Automatically hides distractions on YouTube and can pause videos when it's time for a break.
